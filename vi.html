Velox 1.0

Velox 1.0 was one of the top 65 teams among 120 who were qualified for the fabrication process after submitting their Report analysis atAnsys,Pune.They bagged the Ansys CAE award for best Computer Aided Design.Further, they were one among the 25 teams that qualified for the racing event at Buddh International circuit. The first SUPRA SAEINDIA event was held in the year 2012 sponsored by Maruti Suzuki.

Technical Specification:

Engine:Maruti 800 BS III
Suspension : Direct Actuated Un-Equal Double A-Arm
Brake : Parallel hydraulic actuated 4 wheel disc brakes
Frame : 4130 chromoly steel tubes, TIG welded
Steering : Rack and Pinion, parallel- team manufactured
Tires : 165/60 R13 73H

Achievments:

1.1st place in Ansys CAE Award for best Computer Aided Design
2. Placed 6Th in Acceleration event.
3. 9th in Skidpad that tests the stability of the vehicle.
4. 9th in Autocross-a race against time that tests car's handling.
5. 9th in Endurance event that tests durability of the car and endurance of the driver.
