Velox 2.0

Following the footsteps of the initiators, Velox 2.0 also achieved new heights. After clearing the virtual round held in KIIT Bhubaneshwar, the team was one among the 87 teams who managed to arrive with a complete car. The team cleared the technical inspections with ease on the very first day. Terasvin was the only team to clear the brake test in a single attempt, a fete achieved by no other team.

Technical Specification:

Engine : Honda CBR 600RR
Suspension : Push Rod Activated Unequal Double A-Arm
Brakes : Parallel Hydraulic Actuated 4 Wheel Ventilated Petal Disc
Frame : 4130 Chromoly Steel, TIG Weldedv
Steering : Rack and Pinion, Parallel (Team Manufactured)
Tire : 185/60 R13

Achievments:

1. Won 1st place in Acceleration, a crucial aspect for every racing vehicle (75m @5.49 sec).
2. Placed 2nd in Autocross event.
3. Best time of 3.22 seconds during the Ergonomics test that tests the drivers' interaction with the car.
4. A record 60kmph to 0kmph within 3meters during the Brake test.
5. Placed overall 8th out of 87 participants.
