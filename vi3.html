Velox 3.0

Velox 3.0 with all passion for the event and inspiration from the predecessors has now concentrated on weight reduction so as to accelerate faster. The rest will be future!

 

Technical Specification:

Engine : Yamaha R6
Suspension : Double A-Arm pushrod actuated
Brakes :Ventilated Petal discs
Frame : Chromoly 4130 space frame
Steering : Rack and pinion
Transmission : 6 Speed sequential cable operated
